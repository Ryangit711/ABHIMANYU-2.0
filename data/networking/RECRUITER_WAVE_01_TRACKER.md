## REPLY HANDLER PROTOCOL

**When any reply arrives (auto-reply or real person):**

1. **CLASSIFY** — Auto-reply (out of office, system confirmation) vs Real person (named signature, personal text)
2. **IF AUTO-REPLY** — Log in tracker, set follow-up after OOO date. No action needed.
3. **IF REAL PERSON** — Read their full message. Extract: tone (warm/cold/neutral), action (ask for CV, ask for call, ask for more info, rejection), timeline mentioned.
4. **ALCHEMIZE** — Run Live Intelligence sweep: check their LinkedIn, recent firm news, practice area updates, any shared context from the original email.
5. **RESPOND** — Within 4 hours. Semantic-symbiotic: weave their language + firm intelligence + Aman's scope. Never template. Every response unique to that person's specific reply.
6. **LOG** — Update this tracker with: date replied, tone, outcome, next action.

**FALLBACK — If uncertain about response:** Reply with:
> *"Thank you for the reply. I appreciate you taking the time. [Brief 1-sentence acknowledgment of what they said]. I'm available for a call at your convenience — just let me know what works."*
