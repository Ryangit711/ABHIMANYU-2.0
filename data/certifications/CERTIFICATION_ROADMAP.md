# Certification Roadmap — Free First, Paid Later

**Philosophy:** Start free. Get the credential on LinkedIn. Then invest in the ones that matter.

---

## Phase 1: FREE (Start This Week)

### 1. Google Project Management Certificate
- **Where:** Coursera (audit mode — free)
- **Time:** 10 hrs/week × 6 months
- **Value:** Google name = instant credibility. Agile, Scrum, Waterfall, budgeting, risk. Counts toward CAPM/PMP education hours.
- **Action:** Enroll TODAY. Put "Currently Pursuing: Google Project Management Certificate" on LinkedIn.
- **Link:** https://www.coursera.org/professional-certificates/google-project-management

### 2. Six Sigma Green Belt
- **Where:** Simplilearn SkillUp (free)
- **Time:** 7 hours self-paced
- **Value:** DMAIC, process improvement, root cause analysis. Every ops JD wants this.
- **Action:** Complete this weekend.
- **Link:** https://www.simplilearn.com/free-signal-process-course-skillup

### 3. Excel for Business
- **Where:** Coursera, Macquarie University (audit mode — free)
- **Time:** 4 weeks
- **Value:** Shows Excel proficiency honestly. Not claiming expert — showing competence.
- **Action:** Start alongside Google PM.
- **Link:** https://www.coursera.org/learn/excel-for-business

### 4. LinkedIn Learning (Free with Library Card)
- **Where:** Most Canadian public libraries give free access
- **Courses to take:**
  - Agile Foundations
  - Stakeholder Management
  - Business Process Mapping
  - Change Management Foundations
- **Value:** LinkedIn badges visible on profile. Shows continuous learning.
- **Action:** Get Vancouver Public Library card (free). Access LinkedIn Learning through library.

---

## Phase 2: LOW-COST ($50-$300)

### 5. CAPM (Certified Associate in Project Management)
- **Where:** PMI (Project Management Institute)
- **Cost:** $325 (PMI member) or $395 (non-member)
- **Prereq:** Google PM cert covers the 23 education hours needed
- **Value:** Gold standard for project management. Consulting-ready.
- **Timeline:** After completing Google PM cert (Month 6)

### 6. LinkedIn Learning Premium (Optional)
- **Cost:** $0 (30-day trial) or free with library card
- **Value:** More courses, certificates of completion, LinkedIn badges

---

## Phase 3: INVESTMENT ($500-$2000)

### 7. PMP (Project Management Professional)
- **Where:** PMI
- **Cost:** $555 (PMI member)
- **Prereq:** 36 months project management experience + 35 hours education
- **Value:** Universal credential. Consulting-ready. Required by many Big4 firms.
- **Timeline:** After 6 months in first role (once you have 36 months total experience)

### 8. Prosci Change Management Certification
- **Cost:** ~$1,500
- **Value:** Change management is in every consulting JD. This is the gold standard.
- **Timeline:** After PMP or during first consulting role
