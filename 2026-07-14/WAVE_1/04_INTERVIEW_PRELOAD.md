## PART 1: QUICK REFERENCE — CM FRAMEWORKS

### ADKAR Model (PROSCI)

| Stage | What It Means | Your Story Example |
|-------|---------------|-------------------|
| **A**wareness | Understanding WHY change is needed | *"Staff knew our paper system was failing — wait times increasing, billing errors mounting. Created awareness through all-hands meetings showing cost of inaction."* |
| **D**esire | Motivation to participate | *"Connected the change to their daily pain — scheduling chaos, double-entry. Showed them the new system would make their jobs easier, not harder."* |
| **K**nowledge | How to change (training) | *"Hands-on training sessions across 32 locations. Customized for each role — clinicians, billers, front desk."* |
| **A**bility | Implementing the change (practice) | *"Staged rollout: pilot at 3 clinics first, iterate, then full deployment. Support team on-site for first 2 weeks at each location."* |
| **R**einforcement | Sustaining the change | *"KPI dashboards tracking adoption metrics. Managers reviewed weekly. Re-training for locations lagging behind targets."* |

### Kotter's 8-Step Model

| Step | Your Parallel |
|------|--------------|
| 1. Create urgency | *"Showed that patient attrition was costing $4M/year — inaction cost jobs"* |
| 2. Build guiding coalition | *"Formed steering committee: 1 rep per clinic group + CEO + ops lead"* |
| 3. Form strategic vision | *"One digital ecosystem — no more paper, no more double-entry"* |
| 4. Enlist volunteer army | *"Champions at each location who tested the system first, trained peers"* |
| 5. Enable action by removing barriers | *"Pulled old hardware, solved connectivity issues, fought for budget"* |
| 6. Generate short-term wins | *"First 3 clinics showed 20% reduction in no-shows within 30 days"* |
| 7. Sustain acceleration | *"Used win data to convince remaining 29 locations — momentum built itself"* |
| 8. Institute change | *"New systems became SOP. Old paper processes formally retired at 12-month mark. Monitored regression for 6 months after."* |
