There's a difference between knowing how change management works and knowing how change feels.

[NAME] knows both.

They studied ADKAR in a classroom at KPU. They learned Kotter's 8 steps in their MBA. But they didn't test those frameworks on a case study — they tested them across 32 locations, 5 acquisitions, and 70 employees.

When SkyflyMD acquired its first clinic group, [NAME] didn't just build an integration plan. They talked to every department head about what the change meant for their team, built champions in each group, and tracked adoption through dashboards that showed exactly where change was sticking and where it wasn't. Seaspan is building a $3.15B polar icebreaker. They're launching the longest naval ship ever built in Canada. They're integrating robotics, digital systems, and new processes into a 138-year-old operation.

They don't need a change management specialist who's only ever facilitated workshops.
