### Key STAR Stories

**STAR 1 — Implementation/Configuration**
- **Situation:** New clinic acquired by our network. Existing workflows were chaotic — no SOPs, inconsistent EHR use, staff using different processes across locations.
- **Task:** Standardize all clinical and operational workflows into the EHR, train the team, and achieve consistent execution within 60 days.
- **Action:** Led business discovery with clinic leadership — documented every process (scheduling, messaging, referrals, insurance). Configured eCW with standard templates, user permissions, and protocol rules. Built training program with hands-on sessions. Established quality checks with feedback loops.
- **Result:** Full standardization achieved in 45 days. Team executed consistently across all workflows. Error rate dropped to near zero within 90 days.

**STAR 2 — Change Management**
- **Situation:** New controlled substance regulations required all patients on scheduled medications to complete in-person visits before refills. This was a massive change for 12,000+ active patients and 60+ providers.
- **Task:** Implement the change with zero compliance issues and minimal patient disruption.
- **Action:** Analyzed patient records to identify affected population. Designed communication workflow (letter → call → SMS). Configured EHR to flag controlled substance patients at check-in. Trained front desk and clinical teams on new protocol. Built escalation process for exceptions.
- **Result:** 100% compliance from Day 1. Zero controlled substance violations. Patient complaints were under 2%.

**STAR 3 — Business Discovery / Project Execution**
- **Situation:** MD noticed 10,000+ inactive patient records — patients who hadn't visited in 12+ months. Revenue was below capacity.
- **Task:** Design and execute a patient re-engagement campaign that brings active patients back to the clinic.
- **Action:** Analyzed records to identify viable targets (excluded moved/deceased). Designed multi-channel outreach workflow (call + email + Curegram message). Coordinated scheduling, provider matching, and visit follow-up. Tracked ROI per channel.
- **Result:** Thousands of appointments booked from reactivated patients. Contributed to revenue growth from <$1M to $4M+.
